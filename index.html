About PIE

PIE- Promotion of Innovation and Entrepreneurship is a Special Interest Group of IEEE Kerala Section, aimed in nurturing the entrepreneurship & innovation skills among the students and professional community.

The event is a platform to experiment and innovate.From the ideation till the launch we help you grow your idea through each phase and flourish. We wish to give an insight into the successful marketing strategies which help the startups get market validation for their products.
